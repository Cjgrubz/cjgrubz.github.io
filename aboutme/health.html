When it comes to my health I am a diabetic so my health is important to me. I have had to learn how to control what I eat so I can manage my A1c. It is a big lifestyle change when you are told you are diabetic. But I am taking action to control my glucose levels, I regularly exercise and control my diet to manage my diabetes. I plan on having a sleeve gastrectomy (weight loss surgery) soon. Some may say that's the easy way but at least it is a way to gain some health back. The key is maintaining a good eating habit after the surgery. If not one can find themselves back at square 1. Just a little food for thought.
