One of our other favorite trips is going on family camping trips. I have camped all over the Sierra Nevadas. Some of my favorite places to go camping within a couple hour drive are like Edison lake, Dinkey creek, florence lake just to name a few. There is nothing like getting up in the morning smelling fresh nature instead of auto exhaust and gas fumes. Then spending the day hiking and fishing and exploring. Then the campfires at night making smores, roasting hotdogs over the campfire. These are all experience's that everyone should experience at least once in there life.
