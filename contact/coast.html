Another of my favorite vacations is going to the coast. I've been all up and down the entire west coast from the top of Washington all the way to Baja California. I've also been down the east coast from Rhode Island down to Florida. I personally enjoy the west coast better The views in California I think are the most beautiful. The east coast is nice but it's just not Cali and I'm saying this without prejudice. Besides that the weather is nicer than Florida, yeah it's sunny in Florida but it is also very humid and that I can do without.
