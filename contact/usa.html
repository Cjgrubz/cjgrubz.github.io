One of the other memorable vacations I've taken is the big drive. I've driven from California all the way to the Statue of Liberty on Liberty Island in New York City, New York. It is a very long drive but the sites you see on the way make every mile worth it. I've made this drive more than once, I've driven it so much I have my favorite stops in every state all along the way like the Devils backbone in Utah, Little America Wyoming, Vail pass in Vail Colorado. Then I love driving the plain states at night so I can see the lightning storms that light up across the Midwest. That drive I would recommend to anyone to do at least once in their life.
